SAN FRANCISCO COUNTY TRANSPORTATION AUTHORITY

Uber and Lyft in San Francisco

You're looking at an estimate of the number of TNC (Uber and Lyft) pickups and dropoffs in San Francisco—by location and by time of day.
  • Uber and Lyft trips are combined.
  • Only rides that are entirely within the city limits are counted.
  • Data is averaged from several weeks in fall 2016.
  • Darker colors & taller bars mean more trips to/from an area.
  • The geographic units on the map are "Transportation Analysis Zones" (TAZs), which are at the block level in the downtown core and are groups of blocks farther out.
  • No personal data is shown. Pickups/dropoffs are merged by TAZ.

How to use this map

Click on any block to see the daily pickups and dropoffs for an area. You can choose different days of the week and times of day from the right panel.
